Awards & Judging

Learn about all 24 trophies, how judging works, and what it takes to bring home an award at the L24 Cincy Car, Truck & Bike Show.

Best of Show

Highest-scoring vehicle in the show.

People's Choice

People's Choice is voted on by registered vehicle participants only. Each registered vehicle entry receives one ballot, and participants may not vote for their own vehicle.

Judging Information

  • Vehicles may receive only one trophy.
  • Vehicles must remain until awards are presented to qualify.
  • Judges' decisions are final.
  • People's Choice is voted on by registered vehicle participants only. Each registered vehicle entry receives one ballot, and participants may not vote for their own vehicle.
  • Ties are broken by the judging panel.
