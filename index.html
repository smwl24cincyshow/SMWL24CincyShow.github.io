ABOUT THE SHOW

The SMART Local 24 Car, Truck & Bike Show brings together custom vehicles, motorcycles, local union pride, family fun and community support all in one event. Enjoy trophies, raffles, awards, food, music and an incredible collection of vehicles from around the region while supporting Local 24 and our local community.
