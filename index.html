About The Show

The L24 Cincy Car, Truck & Bike Show brings together union members, local businesses, automotive enthusiasts, families, and the surrounding community for a day of vehicles, fellowship, and support for the skilled trades.

Proceeds from this event help support local apprentices entering the sheet metal trade while strengthening community connections throughout Cincinnati.
